
## PRD Addendum for Upingi.com: Adsense Approval

### Objective
Revise Upingi.com to ensure compliance with Google AdSense content quality requirements, focusing on original, substantial, and user-engaging content.

### Key Issues to Address
1. **Lack of Original, Unique Content**
2. **Content Depth and Quality**
3. **Minimal User Engagement**

### Requirements for Cursor.ai Implementation

#### 1. Original Content Expansion
- Create a dedicated "Blog" or "Articles" section accessible via the top navigation.
- Generate at least 20 original articles (minimum 800 words each) covering topics such as:
  - Software reviews with detailed analysis, personal insights, and user benefits.
  - Software tutorials or step-by-step guides.
  - Industry insights and software comparison articles.
- **Cursor.ai Specific Instruction**: Ensure each article includes unique insights, practical examples, screenshots, or mockups to demonstrate depth clearly.

#### 2. Product Pages Enhancement
- Extend existing product pages to include comprehensive details beyond basic features:
  - Detailed software reviews (pros, cons, use cases).
  - Clear and explicit instructions for downloading, installing, and troubleshooting the software.
  - User tips and recommendations to enhance user value.
- **Cursor.ai Specific Instruction**: Include at least 300 additional words of original content per product page, structured with clear headings (e.g., "How to Install," "User Tips," "Common Issues").

#### 3. User Engagement Features
- Add comment sections at the end of every article and product page to encourage user interaction and demonstrate active community engagement.
- Implement a simple feedback mechanism (e.g., thumbs up/down) for content usefulness at the bottom of each page.
- **Cursor.ai Specific Instruction**: Provide clean and unobtrusive designs for user-engagement tools. Prioritize functionality and ease of use.

#### 4. SEO and Content Quality Checks
- Ensure all content meets Google's Webmaster guidelines, specifically:
  - Unique, human-readable content.
  - Structured data markup for articles (using JSON-LD).
  - SEO-friendly headlines and meta descriptions for each new piece of content.
- **Cursor.ai Specific Instruction**: Automatically generate SEO meta tags and schema.org structured data for each article.

#### 5. Avoiding Overly Aggressive Ad Integration
- Clearly differentiate download buttons from ads. Include explicit labeling (e.g., “Ad” or “Sponsored Link”) on ad units to comply with Google AdSense policies.
- Ensure no deceptive ad placements or designs (ads should be distinctively recognizable).
- **Cursor.ai Specific Instruction**: Adjust ad button styling slightly (e.g., border, shading, or clear labeling) to meet compliance without significantly impacting overall design appeal.

### Implementation Steps for Cursor.ai
- First, expand the site with the new "Blog/Articles" section.
- Enhance existing product pages with richer content.
- Implement user engagement and feedback mechanisms.
- Adjust the visual clarity of ads versus actual download buttons.
- Conduct an SEO and content-quality compliance check.

### Evaluation
Regularly audit the content produced by Cursor.ai for uniqueness and compliance with Google AdSense guidelines, adjusting the guidelines given to Cursor.ai as necessary based on observed shortcomings or compliance issues.
