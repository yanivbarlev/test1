Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 25, 2025

This privacy policy explains how akaqa.com uses the personal data we collect from you when you use our website.

What Data Do We Collect?

You directly provide akaqa.com with most of the data we collect. We collect and process data when you:

  • Register online or download software from our platform.
  • Voluntarily complete a user survey or provide feedback on our website.
  • Use or view our website via your browser’s cookies.

The data we collect may include your name, email address, IP address, browsing history on our site, and preferences related to software downloads.

How Do We Use Your Data?

akaqa.com uses your data to:

  • Improve your experience on our platform by personalizing software recommendations.
  • Process your downloads and manage your account.
  • Send you information about software updates, new tools, and services we think you might like, such as productivity apps, utilities, or security software.
  • Analyze website usage to enhance our services and ensure a seamless download experience.

If you agree to receive marketing emails, you may opt out at any time. You have the right to stop akaqa.com from contacting you for marketing purposes by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email or contacting us directly.

How Do We Store Your Data?

akaqa.com securely stores your data using state-of-the-art encryption technology. We retain cookies and browsing data from your browser for one month. After this period, we automatically delete your data to protect your privacy. We do not share your personal information with third parties unless required by law or to provide services on our behalf (e.g., hosting providers), and only under strict confidentiality agreements.

What Are Your Data Protection Rights?

akaqa.com is committed to ensuring you are fully aware of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

  • The Right to Access: You can request copies of your personal data.
  • The Right to Rectification: You can request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate or incomplete.
  • The Right to Erasure: You can request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The Right to Restrict Processing: You can request that we restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The Right to Object to Processing: You can object to our processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The Right to Data Portability: You can request that we transfer your data to another organization or directly to you, under certain conditions.

If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at: support@akaqa.com.

Cookies

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard internet log information and visitor behavior information. We use cookies to:

  • Track your activity on akaqa.com to improve user experience.
  • Understand your preferences for software downloads.
  • Ensure the security of your browsing session.

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. For more information on cookies, visit our Cookie Policy page (if applicable).

Links to Other Websites

The akaqa.com website contains links to other websites, such as software developer pages or external download sources. Our privacy policy applies only to our website. If you click on a link to another website, we recommend reading their privacy policy.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

akaqa.com keeps its privacy policy under regular review and will place any updates on this web page. This privacy policy was last updated on April 2, 2025.

How to Contact Us

If you have any questions about akaqa.com’s privacy policy, the data we hold on you, or would like to exercise one of your data protection rights, please do not hesitate to contact us at: support@akaqa.com.
