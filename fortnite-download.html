Description

Fortnite is a free-to-play battle royale game where 100 players fight to be the last one standing. Featuring unique building mechanics, regular content updates, and cross-platform play, Fortnite combines shooting action with strategic building. The game also offers various game modes, seasonal events, and collaborations with popular entertainment franchises.
