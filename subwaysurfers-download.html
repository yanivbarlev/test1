Subway Surfers

Category: Games

Price: Free (with in-app purchases)

Developer: Kiloo

Description

Subway Surfers is an endless runner game where you play as a graffiti artist being chased by a grumpy inspector and his dog. Run, jump, and slide through subway tracks while collecting coins, power-ups, and avoiding obstacles. With regular updates featuring new cities, characters, and challenges, Subway Surfers offers endless entertainment for players of all ages.

Features

  • Simple swipe controls
  • Multiple character options
  • Various power-ups and abilities
  • Regular updates with new cities
  • Global leaderboards
  • Daily challenges and missions
  • Character customization
  • Multiple environments to run through
  • Achievement system
  • Social sharing features
