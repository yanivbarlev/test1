Evernote

Category: Productivity

Price: Free (Basic plan)

Developer: Evernote Corporation

Description

Evernote is your second brain for capturing, organizing, and sharing your ideas. Whether you're taking notes, creating to-do lists, or saving web articles, Evernote helps you keep everything organized and accessible across all your devices. With powerful search capabilities and flexible organization tools, you'll never lose an important thought again.

Features

  • Capture notes, ideas, and to-do lists
  • Save web articles and screenshots
  • Organize with notebooks and tags
  • Powerful search across all content
  • Sync across all devices
  • Share notes and collaborate
  • Scan and digitize documents
  • Web clipper for saving articles
  • Offline access to notes
  • Cross-platform compatibility
