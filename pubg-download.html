Description

PUBG (PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds) is a battle royale game where 100 players drop onto a massive map, gather resources, and fight to be the last person standing. With realistic graphics, intense gameplay, and strategic elements, PUBG offers an immersive survival experience. Choose your landing spot, find weapons and equipment, and outmaneuver your opponents in this competitive multiplayer game.
